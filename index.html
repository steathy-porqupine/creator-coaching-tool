‚ö†Ô∏è Creator Mistakes to Avoid

These are the fastest ways to lose subs and income. Don't do these.

‚ùå Mistake 1: Messaging Everyone the Same Way

A super fan who tipped $100 gets the same generic mass DM as someone who lurks silently.

Fix: Segment. Tier 1 gets personalized. Tier 3 gets light mass messages.

‚ùå Mistake 2: Asking for Money Before Trust

First DM exchange is "hey, interested in a custom?"

Fix: Chat 2-3 times. Let them see you're a real person. THEN mention paid options.

‚ùå Mistake 3: Ignoring DMs from Subs

When someone messages first, they're raising their hand saying "I'm interested!" Ignoring them tells them they don't matter.

Fix: Reply to EVERY DM within 24 hours, even if just "Thanks so much! üñ§"

‚ùå Mistake 4: Overpromising Response Time

"I reply to every message in an hour!" then you disappear for days.

Fix: Say "I usually get back within 24 hours." Under-promise, over-deliver.

‚ùå Mistake 5: Being Inconsistent

Message daily one week, disappear for two weeks, then spam 5 messages on Friday.

Fix: Pick a schedule (3 messages/week) and stick to it consistently.

‚ùå Mistake 6: Guilt-Tripping or Shaming

"Everyone else is supporting me. Why aren't you? üò¢"

Fix: Make buying optional. "If you want to support, here's a way" not "you should feel bad if you don't."

‚ùå Mistake 7: Not Saying Thank You

Someone tips or buys and you never acknowledge it.

Fix: Always say thanks. Can be 1-2 words + emoji. Takes 5 seconds.

‚ùå Mistake 8: Vague Offers

"NEW CONTENT!! Check it out üî•üî•" (subs don't know what's new or if it's worth their time)

Fix: Be specific. "Posted a new [content type]‚Äîit's more [vibe] than usual."
